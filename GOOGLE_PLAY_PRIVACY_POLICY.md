# Google Play Privacy Policy for FoodDeli

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Nastasia Food collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use the FoodDeli app available on Google Play.

## Data Collection and Usage

FoodDeli collects the following types of information:

### Information You Provide
- Account information (name, email address, phone number)
- Delivery address information
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
- Profile information (profile picture if added)

### Information Collected Automatically
- Location information (used for delivery purposes only)
- Device information (device type, operating system, unique identifiers)
- Usage information (app interactions, features used, time spent)
- IP address and browser type

## Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and use your information for the following purposes:
- To create and maintain your account
- To process and fulfill your food orders
- To facilitate delivery to your location
- To communicate with you about your orders
- To improve our app and services
- To provide customer support
- To detect and prevent fraud

## Data Sharing

We may share your information with:
- Restaurants and food establishments for order fulfillment
- Delivery partners to facilitate order delivery
- Payment processors to handle transactions
- Service providers who assist us in operating our app
- Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by law

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:
- Encryption of data in transit
- Secure storage of sensitive information
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls to limit data access

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
- The right to access and update your information
- The right to deletion of your information
- The right to data portability
- The right to withdraw consent

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Compliance

This app complies with the Google Play Developer Policy and applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union.
