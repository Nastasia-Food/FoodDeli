# Funding

This document describes how to support the development of FoodDeli through funding.

## About Funding

FoodDeli is an open-source project developed and maintained by the community. Your financial support helps ensure the continued development, maintenance, and improvement of the application.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my contribution tax-deductible?
Contributions through Open Collective may be tax-deductible depending on your jurisdiction. Please consult with a tax professional.

### Can I sponsor specific features?
Yes, sponsors can request specific features or improvements. Priority will be given based on sponsorship level.

### What happens if funding goals aren't met?
Development will continue at a reduced pace, focusing on critical maintenance and community-driven features.

### How are funds distributed?
Funds are distributed based on contributor agreements and project needs. Infrastructure costs are prioritized, followed by development support.
